Bio

Lindsey Krause is a long-time advocate of utilizing the arts across disciplines and fields to enhance wellness and promote social justice. Over the past few years she has been keeping herself busy building an art center from the ground up for a non profit organization in Phoenix, AZ (Thunderbird Arts Center).
